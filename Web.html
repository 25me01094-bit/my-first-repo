About NSS :-

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a government-backed, voluntary program in India under the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports, established in 1969 to provide students with opportunities for community service, focusing on developing their character and leadership skills through hands-on experience with community issues. The scheme aims to connect students with the needs of the rural and urban poor, fostering social awareness, national integration, and responsible citizenship.

Our objectives :-

The objectives of India's National Service Scheme (NSS) aim to develop student leaders through voluntary community service, fostering social and civic responsibility, leadership, and democratic values, all while promoting national integration and social harmony. NSS volunteers learn about their communities, identify needs, find practical solutions, develop skills in mobilization and group living, and gain the ability to respond to emergencies. The scheme seeks to establish meaningful connections between the campus and the community and to put education into social use.
